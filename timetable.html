Shri Vaishnav Polytechnic

Shri Vaishnav Polytechnic, a premier institute of its kind, was established in 1962, under the Open Door Policy of Govt. of India. The institute is managed by Shri Vaishnav Sahayak Kapda Market Technical Education (SVSKMTE) Society Indore. In the last 56 years, the Institute has successfully scaled new heights of achievements in the field of technician education and has earned the reputation of being one of the top ranking polytechnics in the country..
